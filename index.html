About us

Oboodi Barristers & Solicitors was established in 2000 by Ms. Sousan Oboodi. Sousan Oboodi graduated in 1979 from Tehran University and practiced law in Iran before moving to Australia in the late 1980s. She continued her studies in Melbourne University in Australia and completed her law degree in 1995. She has since been working and has been a registered migration agent since 1996. She began her work with a firm of solicitors before establishing her own practice in 2000.
